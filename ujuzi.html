Microsoft Dynamics NAV

Microsoft Dynamics NAV is a business solution from Microsoft that delivers comprehensive business management functionality for small and midsize businesses. A unique combination of business intelligence, collaboration and communication tools connects the many moving parts of your organization, giving you better visibility into and control over what’s going on in your business.

Microsoft Dynamics NAV enables you to make proactive and informed decisions that have a direct impact on your productivity and bottom line—increasing margins and improving your cash flow. Simple to learn and use, Microsoft Dynamics NAV business management software works with your existing technology and Microsoft productivity tools and scales as you grow to deliver long-term value.

 

Dive Into The Capabilities

Quick to implement and easy to use, with the power to support your growth ambition, Microsoft Dynamics NAV delivers comprehensive capabilities to help small and midsize companies manage their entire business.

  • Financial management and accounting: Manage your cash, assets, and banking.
  • Supply chain, manufacturing, and operations: Track and manage your production, inventory, orders, and vendors.
  • Marketing, sales, and service: Manage your campaigns, sales opportunities, contacts, and service contracts.
  • Project management: Create estimates, track projects, and manage capacity.
  • Business intelligence and reporting: Get real-time visibility and analytics to track performance and to act with insight.
  • Multi-currency: Compete globally with multi-currency and language capabilities.

Microsoft Sharepoint

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations (formerly AX) is the leading cloud ERP solution built to make it easier to do business across locations and countries.

Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations is a comprehensive business and financial management solution for medium and large-sized organisations.

With Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations, you can be confident your business management solution is—and will continue to be—relevant to the needs of your people and the demands of your industry and business.

 

End users benefit from easy to use tools that help to improve individual productivity and effectiveness, which has a direct impact on profitability. Giving people familiar, easy-to-use tools enables them to work quickly and make smart, proactive decisions. Better still it’s complemented by a range of fantastic supporting cloud technologies that help with business intelligence, workflow, designing your own apps and integrating other solutions you use.

 

At an organisational level, executives, managers and employees are empowered with the knowledge they need to make sound business decisions – Dynamics 365 Finance and Operations can deliver the information and the analysis tools they need to make that happen. With the ability to custom-tailor the information people need for their specific responsibilities, Dynamics 365 can drive decision-making and improve productivity throughout an organisation.

SAP

SAP is a multinational software company known for their world-class and neatly integrated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software. ERP software allows organisations to manage business operations and is commonly described as a suite of modular applications that collect and integrate data from various aspects of the business. Based on market capitalisation, SAP is the world’s third largest independent software manufacturer.

The SAP ERP suite contains an enormous number of modules, the main categories being Accounting, Logistics, Human Resources and Technology. Sub-modules include Finance, Controlling, and Sales & Distribution – as just a few examples. Beyond these fundamental models, SAP provides industry-specific add-on solutions. Depending on the organisation’s industry, size and business operations, a client can buy whatever modules are needed.

  • SAP BusinessObjects
  • SAP Hybris Commerce
  • SAP Hybris Sales Cloud
  • SAP Hybris Cloud for Customer Service
  • SAP Hybris Marketing
  • SAP S/4HANA
  • SAP SuccessFactors
  • Concur

Microsoft SQL Server

Our Microsoft SQL Server experts can help you implement, upgrade, and build on the SQL Server platform – whether you’re looking for a high-performance, mission-critical application, enterprise-scale data warehouse or BI solution.

SQL Server solutions have the flexibility of being deployed on premises, in the cloud or in a hybrid environment, and can be managed through a common and familiar tool set. And new tools make it even easier to build back-up and disaster recovery solutions using Microsoft Azure, through an easy route to the cloud for on-premises SQL Server databases. This means you can use existing skills to take advantage of Microsoft’s global data centres.

You can use SQL Server for business and analytics workloads as well as OLTP.

SQL server on-premises

  • Deployment support for on-premises infrastructure (physical or virtual)
  • Support for hybrid deployment

SQL server on Microsoft Azure cloud platform (IaaS)

  • Fully featured Microsoft SQL server on the Azure platform.
  • Image-based deployment with different SQL editions (Enterprise, Standard) to save cost of initial deployment and configuration
  • Cost includes operating and SQL Server licenses
  • Save time in licensing, maintenance, hardware purchase and avoid the hardware refresh cycle.
  • Fully integrated with on-premises infrastructure and data sources.
